Getting Started

Getting started with Task Manager is simple and straightforward. Begin by logging into your account or registering if you're a new user. Create your first task by filling out the task creation form, where you can specify titles, descriptions, and deadlines. Organize your work into sprints for time-bound projects or group related tasks under epics for larger initiatives. Assign tasks to team members and monitor progress through the task list view.

Collaborate effectively using the team features. Claim tasks through the task claim system to manage your workload. Use batch creation for adding multiple tasks at once and check sprint details to track progress. The system automatically notifies you about approaching deadlines and any changes. For additional assistance, visit the help center or contact our support team.

Customize your experience through account settings. Set your notification preferences and manage API tokens for integrations. Utilize sprint planning tools and epic tracking for complex projects. These features will help you streamline task management and enhance team collaboration from the very beginning.

Tasks

The /tasks/ endpoint serves as your task management hub. Create new items via /tasks/new/ with titles, descriptions, and deadlines. View and organize all tasks through /tasks/ listing, which supports filtering and sorting. Detailed task information is available at /tasks/[id]/ for each item.

Manage tasks through dedicated endpoints: update details at /tasks/[id]/edit/, remove tasks at /tasks/[id]/delete/, and claim ownership at /claim-task/[id]/. The system tracks all changes automatically, maintaining complete activity history for audit purposes.

For sprint integration, use /tasks/sprint/add/[sprint_id]/ to associate tasks with work periods. Batch operations through /epic/[epic_pk]/ help manage multiple items efficiently. These structured endpoints provide full control while enabling team collaboration.

Sprints

Sprints are managed through the /sprints/ endpoint, where you can view all active work periods. Create new sprints via /sprints/new/, specifying duration and goals. Detailed sprint planning happens at /sprints/[sprint_id]/, showing all associated tasks and progress metrics.

Modify sprint details through /sprints/[sprint_id]/edit/ to adjust timelines or objectives. Remove inactive sprints at /sprints/[pk]/delete/ to maintain clarity. The system automatically calculates sprint velocity and burn-down metrics for performance tracking.

Connect tasks to sprints using /tasks/sprint/add/[sprint_id]/ for organized execution. Review related tasks through the sprint detail view for workload balancing. These endpoints create a structured Agile workflow for your team.

Learn More about Sprints
Epics

Epics are managed at /epics/, providing overviews of major initiatives. Create new epics through /epics/new/ to group related sprints and tasks. Each epic's details are available at /epics/[pk]/, showing progress across all child items.

Update epic scope and objectives via /epics/[pk]/edit as requirements evolve. Remove completed epics at /epics/[pk]/delete/ to focus on active work. The system maintains cross-epic dependency tracking for complex projects.

For batch operations, use /epic/[epic_pk]/ to manage multiple tasks simultaneously. The epic detail view shows how individual tasks contribute to larger strategic goals, maintaining alignment across your organization.

Contacts

Reach our team through the /contact/ endpoint, designed for all support inquiries and feedback. The contact form collects your message details and contact information, with submissions processed securely. Upon successful submission, you'll be redirected to /contact-success/ for confirmation.

For technical issues, include relevant details like error messages from /tasks/ or /sprints/ operations. Our team typically responds within 24 hours to all inquiries submitted through this channel. The system logs all contact attempts for quality assurance purposes.

Combine this with our documentation at /help/ for comprehensive support. For account-specific issues, have your user information ready from /accounts/profile/. This streamlined process ensures efficient resolution of all inquiries.
